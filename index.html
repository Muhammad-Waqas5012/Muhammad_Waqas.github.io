About Me

Earth Scientist
with a Data Mind

I am a Remote Sensing & GIS researcher passionate about using satellite data and computational methods to understand our changing planet. With an MS in Remote Sensing & GIS (CGPA 3.72/4.0) from Pir Mehr Ali Shah Arid Agriculture University and over five years of industry experience, I combine rigorous academic training with production-grade engineering.

My work spans groundwater depletion monitoring with GRACE gravity data, SAR-based flood mapping, large-scale crop health surveillance using Sentinel imagery, and terabyte-scale geospatial pipeline automation with ArcPy and Python. Currently working as GIS Editor at Neo Space Group in Riyadh on enterprise-scale geospatial workflows for defense and government applications.

I am actively pursuing funded PhD opportunities in Europe, particularly in Earth Observation, hydrology, and climate-driven geospatial analytics.
